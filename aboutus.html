OUR STORY

As a racing boy, my passion for speed ignites every time I step onto the track. The thunderous roar of engines, the smell of burning rubber, and the adrenaline coursing through my veins are what drive me forward. From a young age, I knew that the rush of racing was where I belonged. Every decision I make, every modification I apply to my machine, is a testament to my dedication to the sport. Whether it's fine-tuning the suspension for optimal cornering or tweaking the engine for that extra burst of power, every detail matters in the pursuit of victory. Racing isn't just a hobby; it's a way of life—a relentless pursuit of excellence on the asphalt. And when the lights go green, and I push the pedal to the floor, there's no feeling quite like it—pure, unadulterated freedom in the pursuit of speed.

LEGACY OF EXCELLENCE

Through the years, Racing Boy has carved a distinguished reputation within the motorcycle community in the Philippines. What began as a humble endeavor has evolved into a celebrated brand, deeply intertwined with the lifestyle of riders across the nation. Racing teams and individual enthusiasts alike rely on Racing Boy parts to enhance the performance of their bikes, consistently achieving impressive results on the track. This remarkable success has not gone unnoticed by major motorcycle manufacturers, who have recognized Racing Boy as a valuable partner in the realms of motorsports and product innovation. The endorsement of Racing Boy by esteemed names in the industry speaks volumes, exemplified by the official sponsorship of the Yamaha Factory Racing Team for MotoGP in 2013—a partnership that endures to this day. As a proud Filipino, it fills me with pride to witness Racing Boy's journey of growth and excellence, symbolizing the relentless pursuit of perfection on two wheels.

BRAND AWARDS

Throughout decades of relentless dedication to brand building and the unwavering commitment to delivering superior quality products, Racing Boy has garnered prestigious accolades that testify to its excellence. In the culmination of 2011, Racing Boy proudly clinched its inaugural trophy at the "2nd Asia Success Award," organized by the esteemed International Organizing Committee, Asia Success Inc. This triumph marked a significant milestone as Racing Boy was honored with the esteemed title of "Super Golden Product" in the category of motorcycle rim products, reaffirming its status as a trailblazer in the industry. In the dawn of 2012, Racing Boy once again ascended to the pinnacle of recognition. The illustrious Racing Boy brand was bestowed with the distinguished title of the "11th Asia Pacific Top Excellence Brand" by the esteemed Asia Pacific Excellence Entrepreneur Alliance, an accolade supported by the prestigious Global Business Magazine. These prestigious awards stand as a testament to Racing Boy's unwavering pursuit of excellence and serve as a beacon of inspiration for the brand's enduring legacy of quality and innovation.
